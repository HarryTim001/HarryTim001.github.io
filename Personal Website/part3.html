Plot

After meeting with a CIA agent Willis Huntley, Jason asks for his friend Oliver's location. Willis gives the intel for his friend on one condition: do one of the most memorable missions ever.

Music used in the mission:

After that Jason finds out Oliver is about to be sent off to a buyer, and so he rushes to save his friend. However, he grows more distant from his friends as he begins to enjoy all the killing and leans more towards the Rakyat.

"You know... I never thought I'd be able to kill someone. The first time, it felt wrong. Which is good, right? But now...it feels like winning." -Jason

Another friend of Jason, Keith Ramsay, is already bought by Buck Hughes, an Australian ex-soldier and professional hitman working for Hoyt(Vaas' boss). He also likes boys. Buck promises Jason he will release Keith once he has done a series of treasure hunt for a Chinese ceremonial dagger, the Silver Dragon.

Note: The real Silver Dragon is only found now. The one asked by Citra was just in a dream.

After finding the dagger, Buck lets Jason meet with a traumatised Keith due to torture and implied rape. Getting very furious, Jason decides to finish Buck off.

Hearing his younger brother is aleady killed off, Jason decides to go after Vaas by Citra's persuasion. Here, Vaas says one of the most famous video game quotes of all time.

"Did I ever tell you what the definition of insanity is? Insanity is doing the exact same fucking thing over and over again, expecting shit to change. That is crazy." -Vaas

Gameplay

In Far Cry 3, players can hunt for various wildlife and use their skins to craft larger wallets, ammo pouches, etc. A challenge called Path of The Hunter gives players a special reward to craft the highest level of equipments.
