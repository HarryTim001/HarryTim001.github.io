Plot

Jason is rescued by Dennis, an adopted member of the islands' native Rakyat tribe. Dennis recognizes Jason's potential as a warrior and gives him the Tatau, the tattoos of a Rakyat warrior.

"It is brave to swim in a storm, but to sunbathe on a beach with pirates? Insanity." -Dennis

After buying a gun and exploring the jungle as Dennis said, Jason got information that one of his friends, Daisy, is captured by the pirates in a nearby outpost.

Gameplay

Starting from this mission, players can take back outposts from the red pirates for exp, fast-travel point, and weapon selling machines.

Here are some sick outpost capturing from a talented player.

Players can also climb radio towers and remove their scramblers in order to uncover the map and unlock better weapons in the store. It also gives the player a generous amount of exp.

Plot

Jason learns that his friend Daisy is safe at the house of botanist Dr. Earnhardt, who sells chemicals to the pirates, and thus he thinks his house is the safest spot in the island. He also gives Jason and his friends a cave to hide in which an old and broken ship is situated. Daisy thinks they can repair this ship and escape from the island for good.

Daisy and Dr. Earnhardt

Jason takes on a couple of missions from Dennis in order to get the location of his girlfriend, Liza. After doing so, he finds out that she is being held prisoner at Sunset Cove. Jason heads there to rescue his girlfriend.

"I mean without family, who the fuck are we? There was a time where I would do anything for my sister, you know? First time I ever killed was for my sister. But that was not enough for her." -Vaas

Note: Vaas's sister is Citra

Impressed with Jason's prowess, the Rakyat allow him to be the second outsider to enter their sacred temple. After he returns the Silver Dragon Knife, a Rakyat relic, Jason is initiated into the tribe by the priestess Citra.

Silver Dragon Knife

Will Jason be able to save his still captured friends? How will he work with the Rakyat people to achieve that goal? His journey into madness will unfold in certain ways you wouldn't expect.....
